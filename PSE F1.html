Real Power

P = V × I × cosφ
Real power is the actual usable power consumed by loads. Measured in Watts (W).

Per Unit System

Zpu = Zactual / Zbase
Per unit simplifies power system calculations by normalizing quantities.

Short Circuit Current

Isc = V / Z
Used to determine breaker rating and protection settings.

Power Factor Correction

Qc = P(tanφ1 − tanφ2)
Calculates required capacitor kVAR to improve power factor.
